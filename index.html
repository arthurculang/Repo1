Organismal

A free-form alien sculpture grown by simulation: random nuclei are seeded, connected by a minimum spanning tree, then evolved through several generations of branching, mutation and cross-fusion. Each active tip can mutate direction, fork, die, or reach over and fuse with a neighbor. Antiballs carve real negative space and a noise field wrinkles the surface. The whole cloud is blended into a single watertight mesh by marching cubes — physically unrealizable by any method other than 3D printing.
