Organismal

A multi-pass generative sculpture. Random nuclei are evolved through generations of branching and cross-fusion, then each leaf tip seeds a recursive sub-growth at smaller scale for true fractal self-similarity. Hard-edged polyhedra and interlocking metal rings are unioned with the organic mass; antiballs carve voids; a multi-octave ridged noise wrinkles the surface. The result is a single multi-shell STL printable in metal — and topologically irreproducible by hand.
